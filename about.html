    First things first, I bet you need a little help with my name.

    My name is Jiayi, pronounces as "JA-E", (very closely). If you need further help, here is a tutorial. Jiayi means "plus one" in Chinese. My parents named me this because they were LAZY. Both of their last names are Wang. So they thought, ok, here comes another Wang. And they named me Jiayi.

    I was graduated from Communication University of China majoring Communication, 2010. I then came to US to pursue my Master's degree in Communication, Culture and Technology at Georgetown University in Washington, DC. After graduation, I moved to New York and started my career first as a Reporter in Ming Pao Daily News, which is a Hong Kong based Chinese news paper. After that, I worked at Fox News Channel as a Production Assistant.

    I then continued my study in the technology field by pursueing a degree in Computer Science at Pace University, NY. Where I switched my career path from Media to Technology.

    I am now working on her Masters's degree in Instructional Technology and Media program at Teachers College, Columbia University, NY.
